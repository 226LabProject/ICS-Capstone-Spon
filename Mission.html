Sponsorship/Mission page

Why Are We Doing This?

We want to invite you to a celebration showcasing what we have learned in the Information & Computer Systems Program at Camosun College.

This is a huge moment of pride for us, and we would love to celebrate it in a downtown venue with the community.

To make this happen, we are fundraising and asking for sponsors to help cover the remaining venue and event expenses.

Sponsorship Levels

Level 1 — $150

  • Listed on our “Thank You Sponsors” page in the event booklet (text).

Level 2 — $300

  • Listed on our “Thank You Sponsors” page in the event booklet (logo + text).

Level 3 — $500

  • Listed on our “Thank You Sponsors” page in the event booklet (logo + text).
  • Included in sponsor slideshow with logo and description during the event.
  • Logo included on event signage.

Level 4 — $1,000 (1 sponsor)

  • Listed on our “Thank You Sponsors” page in the event booklet (logo + text).
  • Logo included on event signage.
  • Dedicated page in the event booklet.
  • Special dessert feature on the event day for Key sponsor only.
